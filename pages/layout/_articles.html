Rock Garnet 80 mesh

9.09.2017

Rock Garne 80 is used for both sandblasting and waterjet cutting. It is the most popular fraction, smoother cutting and more even edges than at 30/60, mainly used for cutting tiles, kevlar, marble, granite, stone, copper, aluminum alloy, and other precious metals.
